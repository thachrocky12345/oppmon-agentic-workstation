## What Really Global Is

A **global telehealth technology platform and mental health marketplace** operating in 200+ countries. The platform connects clients with licensed and non-licensed mental health and wellness practitioners (therapists, psychologists, nurse practitioners, social workers, coaches, mentors) via:

- **Transparent search** with 700+ filtering options
- **Instant booking** with real-time calendar integration
- **Talk Now** — on-demand, no-wait session access

Key differentiator: **direct-pay, no insurance required**. Providers keep 80–95% of session revenue. Platform is free to join.

---

## Three Audiences

### 1. Clients
People blocked from care by cost, insurance barriers, geography, or wait times. Use case examples:
- Trauma therapy that fits their budget
- Career coaching on-demand
- Talk Now for immediate anxiety support

### 2. Care Providers (Practitioners)
Mental health and wellness practitioners who want:
- Practice freedom (no insurance, no rate controls)
- Global reach (clients across time zones)
- Supplemental or full-time income
- No subscriptions or monthly fees — earn from session one

### 3. Organizations *(launching 2026)*
Companies, schools, nonprofits providing employee wellness benefits without:
- Insurance complexity
- Limited local provider networks
- Difficulty measuring outcomes across global populations

## Provider Value Props

- Keep **up to 95%** of session revenue (5% in-person fee / 15% telehealth fee)
- Telehealth drops to **12%** above monthly GMV threshold (loyalty tier)
- **Full payment** for late cancellations and no-shows
- Calendar sync: Google, Apple, Microsoft — availability updates everywhere
- SEO-optimized profile with unlimited media
- AI-powered search surfaces profile to pre-filtered, ready-to-book clients
- "Talk Now" instant availability toggle
- Certn.co background verification for credibility
- Direct booking links shareable anywhere
- Free to join — no subscriptions, no hidden fees

## Client Value Props

- **Find Matches** assessment tool — surfaces providers aligned to preferences
- 700+ filtering dimensions (specialty, language, rate, availability, modality)
- AI-powered natural language search ("ADHD coach who's also a parent")
- Real-time calendar booking — confirmed and pre-paid
- **Talk Now** — connect with available providers in seconds
- Verified providers clearly marked and prioritized in search
- No subscriptions, no minimums — pay per session
- Session history stored for easy rebooking
- Works while traveling, relocating, across time zones

## Organization Value Props *(2026)*

- Centralized administration + budget controls
- Utilization analytics with clear ROI data
- Multi-language, multi-time-zone workforce coverage
- No insurance or provider network management required

## What Really Global Is NOT

- Really Global does **not** practice medicine or provide healthcare directly
- Really Global is **not** a therapy/coaching provider — it is a **technology platform**
- Providers ("Companies") are solely responsible for the Mental Health Services they deliver
