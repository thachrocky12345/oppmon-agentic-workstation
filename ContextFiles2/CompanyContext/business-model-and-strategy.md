## Competitive Moat

Five structural advantages:

1. **Liquidity flywheel** — Price/time-zone/language density across 200+ countries + Talk Now creates unbeatable fill rates and booking speed
2. **Hard-to-copy payment rails** — Cross-border compliant payouts in 200+ countries (viable only since 2023), licensure-aware visibility, proven compliance posture
3. **Network effects via affiliates** — All non-licensed providers + clients become distributors at 5% revenue share; organic growth loops competitors can't match without destroying unit economics
4. **Embedded distribution lock-in** — Once LLMs integrate the API/MCP, switching costs compound (re-QA, credentials, safety testing, proven track record)
5. **Programmatic SEO at scale** — 270,000+ pages → default mental health booking layer for LLMs via API/MCP

## Competitor Landscape

Incumbents are constrained by:
- Licensure/geography locks (single country)
- Payer-locked economics → rate caps → provider underpayment → churn
- Closed networks / manual ops → linear (not exponential) scaling
- Product limits (language, time zone, single modality)

**Provider economics comparison:**
- Incumbents: providers keep ~35%
- Really Global: providers keep 80–92%

**Bottom line:** Competitor models expand access only at the margins (insured US users, corporate employees) — they don't unlock new providers, cross borders, or scale care minutes globally.
