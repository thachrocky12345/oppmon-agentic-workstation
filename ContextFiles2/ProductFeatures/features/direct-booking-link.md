## User journey / key actions
- 1. Feature Name - Direct Booking Link
- 2. One‑Paragraph Summary
- This feature introduces provider‑attributed pricing on Really Global. When a client discovers a provider through that provider’s profile (or equivalent off‑site booking entry point) and later books that same provider, the platform fee for that provider–client relationship is reduced from the standard rate to a lower rate. This incentivizes providers to actively share their Really Global presence, aligns platform economics with real demand creation, and builds a long‑term distribution moat without introducing affiliate programs, payouts, or third‑party tracking.
