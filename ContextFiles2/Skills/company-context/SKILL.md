# Skill: company-context

## When to use
Use this skill when you need authoritative answers about:
- What Really Global is, its mission, vision, or purpose
- How the platform works for clients, providers, or organizations
- Fee structure, revenue model, business model details
- Competitive positioning or "why us vs. competitors"
- Market sizing (TAM/SAM/SOM), GTM strategy
- Leadership background
- Official terminology (what is "Company", "Client", "Talk Now", etc.)
- Anything requiring an accurate, non-hallucinated answer about the product or company

## Key facts to have ready

**Fee structure:**
- In-person: 5% platform fee (provider keeps 95%)
- Telehealth: 15% platform fee (provider keeps 85%)
- Loyalty tier: 12% after GMV threshold
- Talk Now: same as telehealth
- Affiliates: 5% commission, both sides, 2-year window

**Core statements:**
- Vision: "connect the world to effective, personalized mental health care"
- Mission: highest quality platform + most diverse providers + utmost convenience
- Purpose: "alleviate suffering and foster well-being for everyone"

**RG is NOT:** a healthcare provider, therapy practice, or insurance platform. It is a technology platform.

**Platform terminology:**
- "Company" = care provider entity (not a corporation)
- "Client" = person seeking services
- "Really" = AI assistant in customer support portal
- "Talk Now" = on-demand instant session toggle

## Steps

1. Read the relevant CompanyContext doc (usually just one)
2. Extract the specific fact, framing, or policy
3. If the answer requires combining product details with company context, also read the relevant ProductFeatures doc
4. Always cite the source doc when answering

## Anti-patterns
- Do NOT guess fee percentages — always read `business-model-and-strategy.md`
- Do NOT describe RG as a therapy provider or healthcare company
- Do NOT use "provider" and "Company" interchangeably without noting the specific policy context
