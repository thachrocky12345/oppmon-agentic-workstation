# Error avoidance tactics

- Validate assumptions with at least two sources (code + docs or config).
- Confirm entry points by tracing references rather than relying on names.
- Record uncertainties explicitly; do not present guesses as facts.
- Recheck diffs for unintended changes or unrelated edits.
- Re-run relevant commands after modifying scripts or configs.
