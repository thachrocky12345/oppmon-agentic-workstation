# Structure tactics

- Scan top-level folders to infer modules, apps, and shared layers.
- Use naming conventions (controllers/services/repos/components) to infer responsibilities.
- Identify cross-cutting concerns (config, logging, auth, metrics) early.
- Note monolith vs. multi-service boundaries and how they communicate.
- Look for seams where old and new frameworks coexist.
