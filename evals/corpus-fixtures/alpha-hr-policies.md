# Alpha Co. HR Policies

This document collects the human-resources policies for Alpha Co.
employees. It covers paid time off, parental leave, remote work, sick
leave, and performance reviews. Owners: People Operations.

## Paid Time Off

Standard PTO accrual is 20 working days per year, in addition to 10
company-observed holidays. PTO accrues monthly and may carry over up to
five days into the next calendar year. Unused balances beyond five days
are forfeited at year end.

## Parental Leave

All parents — birthing, non-birthing, and adoptive — receive 16 weeks
of fully paid parental leave. Leave may be taken in two contiguous
blocks within the first 12 months following the qualifying event.

## Remote Work

Remote work arrangements lasting more than two consecutive weeks
require pre-approval from the employee's direct manager and the People
Operations team. Standard remote stipends apply for equipment.

## Sick Leave

Sick leave is provided at 10 days per year. Unused sick leave does not
roll over into the following year. Sick leave does not require a
doctor's note for absences of three days or fewer.

## Performance Reviews

Performance reviews are conducted twice yearly, in March and September.
Reviews include self-assessment, peer feedback from at least three
colleagues, and a forward-looking development plan.
