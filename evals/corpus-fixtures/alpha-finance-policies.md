# Alpha Co. Finance Policies

This document describes Alpha Co.'s finance and procurement policies.
Owner: Finance Operations. Last revised quarterly.

## Expense Reports

Expense reports must be submitted within 30 calendar days of the
expense being incurred. Reports filed late require an exception
approval from the employee's department head.

## Receipt Requirements

Receipts are required for every individual expense item over $25.
Itemized receipts are mandatory for meals and entertainment regardless
of amount.

## Travel Booking

All business travel must be booked through the company's Concur travel
portal. Direct bookings outside Concur are reimbursed at the lower of
the actual cost or the Concur-quoted rate.

## Procurement Sign-Off

Procurement contracts with a total annual value over $50,000 require
explicit written sign-off from the Chief Financial Officer before the
contract is signed.

## Reimbursement Timeline

Approved reimbursements are processed in the next regular payroll
cycle, typically within two weeks of approval. Direct deposit is the
default payment method.
